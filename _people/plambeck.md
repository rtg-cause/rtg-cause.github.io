Swantje Plambeck is a research assistant and PhD candidate at the Insitute of Embedded Systems at the Hamburg Institute of Technology (TUHH). Her research focus is in model learning for Cyber-Physical Systems with a specific focus on discrete, hybrid, and interpretable models.

## Research Topics
- Cyber-Physical Systems
- Model Learning
- Decision Trees
- Automata Learning
- Hybrid Systems
- System Identification
- Black-Box Testing
